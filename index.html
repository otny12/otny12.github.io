    Green Thumb

    A usability focused webapp for ametuer gardeners wishing to track the weather and how it effects thier plants watering schedules. Me and four other students collaborated through a User Centered Design Model to prototype the design. During discovery direct observation methods were used and two rounds of rapid prototyping with user testing and heuristic reviews were conductted.
